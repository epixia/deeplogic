# DeepLogic — Executive Summary

**One line:** DeepLogic turns an organization's scattered, in-people knowledge into a living, queryable "second brain" — and lets staff capture it, build on it, and ship internal tools against it, all in one platform.

**Status (29 Jun 2026):** H1 2026 platform **delivered end-to-end** ✅ · now hardening & scaling.

---

### The problem we solve
Critical know-how lives in people's heads and scattered files. It's slow to find, easily lost when staff leave, and impossible to build on. DeepLogic captures it, organizes it, and makes it actionable.

### The loop we shipped
**Capture → Organize → Act**

1. **Capture** — AI "Brain Dump" interviews (browser, avatar, voice, phone, **video**) and multi-format Data Vault ingestion pull tacit knowledge in, with attribution.
2. **Organize** — a bi-temporal **knowledge graph** with graph-aware recall (GraphRAG), dedupe and a visual curation UI makes it searchable and trustworthy.
3. **Act** — staff **vibecode internal tools** in sandboxes grounded on that data, and **autonomous agents** run multi-step missions on real cloud computers.

---

### What's delivered ✅

| Capability | Business value |
|---|---|

| **Innovation Lab (vibecoding)** | Staff ship internal tools in minutes, grounded on real data |

### Proof point
A full **Cannara** demo — realistic 3-year sales data, live dashboards, and end-to-end knowledge capture — validates the platform working in a real-world scenario.

### What's next (H2 2026) 🔮

### Dependencies ⚠️
Third-party services are configured and key-gated: avatar (LiveAvatar), voice/phone (Vapi), enrichment (Apollo/Lusha), sandboxes (e2b), agents (Orgo). All keys are managed server-side by admins.
